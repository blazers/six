<span class="headword">Sihtric <span class="st"> [Sigtryggr]</span> inn Gamli</span>  (<em>d.</em> 871), <span class="occ">viking leader</span>, fought and was slain, along with his namesake, <a name="cosubject_25542"></a><span class="headword">Sihtric <span class="st"> [Sigtryggr]</span> inn Ungi</span>  (<em>d.</em> 871), at the battle of Ashdown. Both men were earls (&#145;the Old&#146; and &#145;the Young&#146; respectively) and they were among the five who jointly headed a division of the heathen army during the battle.<br>
<br>Marios Costambeys </div>
