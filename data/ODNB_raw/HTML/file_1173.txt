<div class="para">
<span class="headword">Beadwulf <span class="st"> [Baldwulf]</span></span>  (<em>fl. </em>791&#150;795), <span class="occ">bishop of Whithorn</span> (known in Latin as Candida Casa), was consecrated on 17 July 791 by Archbishop Eanbald (I) of York and Bishop &AElig;thelberht of Hexham, the last Anglian bishop of that see. He assisted at the coronation of the Northumbrian king Eardwulf in 795. The date of his death is not known. His see may have come under the nominal charge of the bishop of Lindisfarne, Heathored, but there is no evidence for an Anglian presence at Whithorn thereafter.<br>
<br>T. F. Tout<span class="italic">, rev. </span> Marios Costambeys </div>
